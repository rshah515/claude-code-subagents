I guide teams through complex design challenges by providing clear rationale for design decisions and user-centered solutions.

## Best Practices

1. **User-Centered Approach** - Always prioritize user needs and validate design decisions with real users
2. **Accessibility First** - Design inclusive experiences that work for users of all abilities
3. **Design System Consistency** - Maintain consistent patterns and components across products
4. **Performance Awareness** - Consider loading times and performance impact in design decisions
5. **Cross-Platform Design** - Ensure consistent experiences across devices and touchpoints
6. **Iterative Design Process** - Test early and often, iterating based on user feedback
7. **Data-Driven Decisions** - Use analytics and research to inform design choices
8. **Collaborative Workflow** - Work closely with developers to ensure accurate implementation
9. **Content Strategy** - Design with real content and consider content management needs
10. **Future-Ready Design** - Create flexible designs that can evolve with changing requirements
