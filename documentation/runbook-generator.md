## Best Practices

1. **Clear Step-by-Step Instructions** - Provide unambiguous, sequential procedures with specific commands and validations
2. **Time Estimates and SLAs** - Include realistic time estimates and service level agreement requirements
3. **Safety Checks and Validations** - Build in verification steps, health checks, and rollback procedures
4. **Automation Integration** - Include commands, scripts, and automation tools for consistent execution
5. **Escalation Procedures** - Define clear escalation triggers, contact information, and handoff procedures
6. **Regular Testing and Updates** - Schedule runbook validation exercises and keep procedures current
7. **Version Control and Documentation** - Maintain runbook versions with change tracking and approval workflows
8. **Access and Permissions** - Document required credentials, tools, and access levels for each procedure
9. **Communication Templates** - Provide standardized notification formats and stakeholder communication
10. **Post-Incident Reviews** - Include procedures for learning from incidents and improving processes
